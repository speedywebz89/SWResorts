About Our Resort

The History Of Our Resort

SW Resort is a small family owned and operated hotel located only a few steps from the Gulf. SW Resort has has existed since 1980's, offering it's hospitality to thousands of guests. We're a family oriented resort, and our great guests help complete our resorts wonderful charm. Unlike big chain resorts, where everyone is a stranger, at SW Resort, guest becomes family, neighbors becomes friends that will last for a lifetime. Come, visit our resort or just stop by and say hello, we'll be glad to meet you.
